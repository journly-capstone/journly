With the rapidly changing environment and increasingly high demands of the profession, many teachers have found themselves over stressed and in search of ways to prevent teacher burnout. That’s where Journly comes in. With Journly, teachers can practice gratitude, be inspired by daily affirmations, and browse through our library of mindfulness books.

Community

We are committed to cultivating a community of educators that support each other along their journey to end the teacher burnout crisis and foster gratitude. That’s why we’ve created the community gratitude board. On this board, users can share gratitude entries with others in the Journly Community. Users can then show their support and appreciation for the post by giving it a "like."
